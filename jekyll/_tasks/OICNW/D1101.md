In order to satisfy  [NVIC 12-14  Task  11.01]({{site.baseurl}}/assets/images/nvic-12-14.pdf), MMA students must:

* Pass MMA Course [MT-2141]( {{site.baseurl}}/courses/MT-2141) *Ship Construction*
* Pass MMA Course [MT-3261]( {{site.baseurl}}/courses/MT-3261) *Containerization and Modern Cargo Stowage*
