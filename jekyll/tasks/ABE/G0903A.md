### MMA Method

In order to satisfy  [NVIC 18-14  Task  9.3.A](/stcw23/assets/images/nvic-18-14.pdf), MMA students must:

* Pass MMA Course  [CC-MANT](CC-MANT) *Shipboard Maintenance*
* Pass MMA Course  [ST-0999D](ST-0999D) *Sea Term I (Deck Portion)*
