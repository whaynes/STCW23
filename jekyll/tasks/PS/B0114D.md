### MMA Method

In order to satisfy  [NVIC 08-14  Task  1.14.D](/stcw23/assets/images/nvic-08-14.pdf), MMA students must:

* Pass MMA Course  [LB-0203](LB-0203) *4/C STCW Immersion Suit Practical*
* Successfully complete MMA Assessment  [PS-2](PS-2) *Don and use an immersion suit*
